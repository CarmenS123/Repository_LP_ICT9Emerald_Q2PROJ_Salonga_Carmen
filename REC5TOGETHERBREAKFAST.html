- 4 Waffles
- Maple syrup (in video it looks more like chocolate so it's an alternative)
- Popcorn
- Whipped Cream
- 1 Strawberry

(Tutorial based off the Original Video)

1.) Toast up some waffles or make your own.

2.) Drizzle syrup all over the waffles.

3.) Microwave your popcorn (if you need to) then sprinkle some onto the top of the waffles.

4.) Decorate the top with some whipped cream then place a strawberry on top of the cream.
