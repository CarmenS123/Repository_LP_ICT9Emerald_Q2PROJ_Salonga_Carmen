- 1 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose or gluten-free flour
- 1 cup of cane sugar
- 3 tbsp of cocoa powder
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 5 tbsp applesauce
- 1 tsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup of cold water

Optional: your choice of toppings for the cake!

(Tutorial located at the end of: Peppa Loves to Bake)

1.) Preheat the oven to 375° F/190° C.

2.) Mix together flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Then add in applesauce, apple cider vinegar, vanilla extract, and water. Mix until smooth.

3.) Pour batter into an 8” x 8” square or 9” round cake pan.

4.) Using an oven mitt, put the cake pan in the oven.

5.) Bake until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 30-40 minutes.

6.) Take the cake out of the oven. Once it’s cooled, decorate however you please!
