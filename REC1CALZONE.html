Dough (for 4 large or 6 small calzones):

- 200g Greek yogurt
- Approximately 2 cups of wheat flour with 2 teaspoons of baking powder (or replace with flour that already comes with baking powder)
- 2 level teaspoons of salt

Filling (quantity to your liking):

- Cubed pepperoni salami (or replace with mortadella or ham)
- Fresh or preserved champignon mushrooms in cubes
- Tomato sauce seasoned with basil
- Coarsely grated mozzarella cheese

Olive oil for brushing the dough (optional)

Quantity: 4 large or 6 small calzones

(Tutorial made by: TomoTchan)

1.) Place the yogurt in the bowl and gradually add the flour and mix until it comes together and forms a ball that doesn't stick to your hands.

2.) Knead for 5 minutes.

3.) Cover with plastic wrap and let it rest in a warm place (inside the microwave) for 1 hour.

4.) Then, open it into a round shape, fill it however you prefer, brush with olive oil and place in a preheated oven at 200ÂºC for around 20 minutes or until baked and golden.
