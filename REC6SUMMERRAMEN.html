Homemade Gum Dare Sauce:

- 1 tablespoon peanut butter (skippy)
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 and 1/2 tablespoons of vinegar
- 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons of sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon chicken broth powder
- Approximately 2-3g of grated garlic (optional)
- 1 tablespoon of water
- 1 tablespoon ground sesame seeds

- Cooked ramen noodles (drain and cool well with ice water)

(Tutorial made by: TomoTchan)

1.) Heat the peanut butter in the microwave (600w / 20 seconds) and mix with a whisk until it becomes soft.

2.) Add sesame oil and mix very well.

3.) Now just add the rest of the ingredients and mix.

4.) Mix the cooked pasta with the sauce and serve with the topping of your choice.
